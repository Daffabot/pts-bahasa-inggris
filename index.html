Latihan Soal Simple Present vs Present Continuous
=======================================================================================
1. A : What's the novel you ... entitled?
B : It's Guru Aini from Andrea Hirata. It's recommended novel to read.
A. Read
B. Have read
C. Are reading

2. She always ... to school on foot.
A. Goes
B. Is going
C. Has gone

3. We ... in an apartement now.
A. Stay
B. Are staying
C. Stayed

4. He never ... a letter to me.
A. Writes
B. Are writing
C. Will write

5. "Could you be quiet, please? -- I ... Math now.
A. Study
B. am studying
C. Studied

6. Koalas ... for most of the day.
A. Sleep
B. Are sleeping
C. Is sleeping

7. Her daughter ... her very often.
A. Is not visiting
B. Does not visit
C. Don't visit

8. Look! The man ... to jump over the bridge.
A. Tries
B. Is trying
C. Tried

9. ... to school every morning?
A. Do you walk
B. Are you walking
C. Did you walk

10. Can you answer the phone? --- I ... .
A. Cook
B. Am cooking
C. Cooked

11. My mentor ... an evaluation every week.
A. Gives
B. Gave
C. Is giving

12. We usually ... swimming at the weekend.
A. Go
B. Went
C. Are going

13. The bank ... on Saturdays.
A. Does not open
B. Is not opening
C. Did not open

14. Sorry, you can't meet him now. He ... now.
A. Sleeps
B. Is sleeping
C. Has slept

15. " Where's Wafiq?"
'He ... basketball with his friends right now.'
A. Plays
B. Is playing
C. Played
